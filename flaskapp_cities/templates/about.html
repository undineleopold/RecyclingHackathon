Individuals face recycling decisions every day. The decisions are particularly challenging when individuals visit new cities where local waste management rules might be different from one another. In 2020, the world generated 2.24 billion tonnes of solid waste; the equivalent of 0.79 kilograms per person per day.

With rapid growth and increased mobility of the population, the global waste is expected to increase by 73% per annum and reach 3.88 billion tonnes in 2050. As 80 % of the waste was not recycled, we believed that by supporting individuals making recycling decisions we can reduce waste. Learn More
